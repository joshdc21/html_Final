our story

MasterSep, a culinary institution with a storied past, traces its origins back to a small family kitchen where the founder, Chef Sebastian Masters, first experimented with flavors that would later define the restaurant. Established in 1995, MasterSep began as a humble eatery, gradually evolving into a celebrated gastronomic destination. The restaurant's name is an amalgamation of "Master" denoting culinary expertise and "Sep," a nod to the founder's sepia-toned memories that inspired his culinary journey. Over the years, MasterSep has become synonymous with innovation, blending traditional recipes with contemporary flair. Through its commitment to quality and passion for culinary artistry, MasterSep has etched its name into the city's culinary landscape, narrating a captivating tale of growth, flavor, and enduring success..
